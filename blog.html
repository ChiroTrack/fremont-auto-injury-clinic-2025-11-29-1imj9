Understanding Whiplash: Causes, Symptoms, and Recovery at Fremont Auto Injury Clinic

Whiplash is a common yet often underestimated auto accident injury caused by rapid neck acceleration and deceleration. Symptoms—including neck pain, headaches, and numbness—may appear hours or days after impact. Early chiropractic treatment is critical for preventing chronic complications like persistent pain and scar tissue formation. Fremont Auto Injury Clinic offers same-day appointments and comprehensive care combining adjustments, physical therapy, and soft tissue treatment. Recovery timelines vary from weeks to months depending on injury severity and individual factors. Prompt intervention significantly improves outcomes and reduces long-term complications. The clinic handles insurance coverage, ensuring accessible treatment for accident victims seeking proper rehabilitation and complete recovery.
