Cookie Policy

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and track your interactions with our site.

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality and security
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our website
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Track website traffic and user behavior for improvement purposes
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver targeted advertising and track campaign effectiveness

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can choose to refuse cookies or receive alerts when cookies are being sent. However, blocking cookies may affect the functionality of our website. For more information about cookies and how to manage them, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.

We also use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information about how visitors use our site. For more information about Google's privacy practices, visit https://policies.google.com/privacy.

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our security practices include:
