Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and treatment

What should I do immediately after a car accident?

First, ensure your safety and the safety of others. Call emergency services if anyone is injured. Then, document the accident scene with photos, exchange information with other drivers, and contact your insurance company. Most importantly, seek medical attention even if you don't feel pain immediately—some injuries like whiplash develop over hours or days. Contact Fremont Auto Injury Clinic as soon as possible for a professional evaluation. We offer same-day appointments to ensure you receive prompt care.

Will my insurance cover chiropractic treatment for auto injuries?

In most cases, yes. Auto insurance policies typically cover chiropractic treatment for injuries sustained in accidents. Our experienced staff works directly with insurance companies to verify your coverage, file claims, and handle billing. We'll help you understand your benefits and ensure you receive the maximum coverage available. You typically won't need to pay out-of-pocket for treatment covered by your policy, though this depends on your specific insurance plan.

How long does recovery typically take?

Recovery time varies depending on the severity of your injuries. Mild injuries may resolve in a few weeks, while more serious injuries could take several months. During your initial consultation, our chiropractors will evaluate your condition and provide an estimated recovery timeline. Most patients begin experiencing significant pain relief within the first 2-4 weeks of treatment. We focus on both immediate symptom relief and long-term healing to ensure complete recovery and prevent chronic issues.

Are chiropractic adjustments safe for auto injury patients?

Absolutely. Chiropractic care is a safe, non-invasive treatment that has been proven effective for auto injury recovery. Our chiropractors are licensed professionals with extensive training in treating accident-related injuries. We conduct thorough evaluations and use advanced diagnostic techniques to ensure that adjustments are appropriate for your specific condition. We may also use complementary therapies like massage, stretching, and rehabilitation exercises to support your recovery safely and effectively.

What if I don't have a referral from my doctor?

You don't need a referral to visit our clinic. As licensed chiropractic professionals, we can evaluate and treat auto injury patients directly. Many patients come to us immediately after an accident without any referral. However, if you'd like to involve your primary care physician in your treatment plan, we're happy to communicate with them and coordinate your care. We work collaboratively with all healthcare providers to ensure comprehensive recovery.
